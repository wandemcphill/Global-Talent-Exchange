# GTEX Terms & Conditions

## Purpose
GTEX is a football fandom, club-building, competition, and digital player-card platform.

## Core Rules
- GTEX competitions are governed by published rules and platform templates.
- Promotional rewards are funded by GTEX promotional pools and not by random wager pools.
- Users must provide accurate account details and keep credentials secure.
- Abuse, fraud, wash trading, match manipulation, or botting may lead to suspension.

## Marketplace
- Player-card marketplace trades attract platform fees as configured by the platform.
- Users may trade with each other subject to policy, fraud checks, and settlement controls.

## Wallet & Withdrawals
- Deposits, balances, and withdrawals are subject to region policy, KYC, and manual or automatic rail controls.
- GTEX may place holds on suspicious transactions.
