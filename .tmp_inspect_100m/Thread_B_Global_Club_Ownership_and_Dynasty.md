# THREAD B — GLOBAL CLUB OWNERSHIP AND CLUB DYNASTY SYSTEM

Build GTEX's club prestige, dynasty, and long-term historical identity layer.

## 1. Club Dynasty System
Every club should accumulate long-term history.

Track:
- trophies
- reputation
- fanbase size
- revenue
- legendary players
- iconic matches
- creator milestones where relevant

### Historical rankings
Support surfaces like:
- Greatest Clubs in GTEX History
- Most Decorated Clubs
- Richest Clubs
- Most Watched Clubs
- Biggest Fanbases

## 2. Club Hall of Fame Expansion
Strengthen club legacy with:
- top scorers
- top appearance makers
- legendary captains
- iconic regens
- dynasty players
- creator-era legends where relevant

## 3. Global Club Ownership / Fan Ownership
Build a governance-ready fan ownership layer.

This should support:
- user clubs
- creator clubs
- special clubs if configured

### Ownership behavior
Fans can acquire ownership stakes or participation units in clubs.

Support:
- ownership ledgers
- fan voting rights on approved club decisions
- governance panels
- revenue/perk distribution rules under admin policy

### Safety
Keep controlling stake protections and admin-configurable caps.

## 4. Data / Models
Add or extend:
- club_dynasty_profiles
- club_historical_rankings
- club_ownership_units
- club_ownership_ledgers
- club_governance_votes
- club_legacy_records

## 5. Tests
Write tests for:
- dynasty stat accumulation
- historical ranking generation
- ownership issuance
- voting rights assignment
- cap/anti-takeover protections

## 6. Non-trample rules
- Do not override previous club share/supporter systems blindly
- Keep governance permissions limited and explicit
- Preserve creator control protections where required
