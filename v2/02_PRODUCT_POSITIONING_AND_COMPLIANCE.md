# Product Positioning and Compliance

## Core positioning
Global Talent Exchange is a **football talent discovery and collectible player card platform** with fantasy competitions, scouting tools, and social market activity.

## Regulatory-safe positioning rules
Use these terms in product copy, docs, onboarding, and policy pages:
- football talent discovery
- collectible player cards
- scouting marketplace
- fantasy competitions
- player development tracking
- platform credits and coins

Avoid these terms unless formal legal review later says otherwise:
- investment
- profit guarantee
- securities
- token sale
- exchange-traded asset
- wallet custody
- stored value account

## Mandatory disclaimer block
Use this in onboarding, payments, terms, and store review notes:

> Coins and credits are virtual units used within the platform. They are not bank deposits, securities, or legal tender. Player cards are digital collectibles used for platform activities, competitions, and discovery features. The platform does not guarantee profits or investment returns.

## KYC and abuse controls
- identity verification required before higher-risk withdrawals or threshold breaches
- anti-fraud review for suspicious payout patterns
- cooldowns on certain trade and listing actions
- immutable ledger for accounting
- webhook-only balance updates for successful payments

## App store / reviewer angle
If asked what the app is, describe it as:
- a football talent discovery app
- a collectible player card platform
- a fantasy competition and scouting experience

Do not describe it as a securities or investment platform.
