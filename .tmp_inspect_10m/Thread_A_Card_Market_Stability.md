- Max copies per player per club = 1
- Elite player limit per club = configurable

Mbappe, Haaland, Osimhen classified as elite tier.

- real world form

- scarcity
- demand

4. Anti Price Manipulation

- same user multi‑account trades
- suspicious price spikes

Admin controls:
- emergency price cooling
- trade monitoring dashboard
