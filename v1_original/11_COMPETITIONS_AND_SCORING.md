# Competitions and Scoring

## Main club competitions
- monthly
- yearly

These should reward smart management, not just rich squads.

## FIFA and national team competitions
Include:
- World Cup
- U20 World Cup
- U17 World Cup
- AFCON
- Euros
- Asian Cup
- other national team competitions as relevant

Rule:
Freeze from first kickoff to final whistle of final.
No new players can be added once competition starts.

## Club competitions
Include:
- UEFA Club Championship
- African Club Championship
- South American Club Championship
- Asian Club Championship
- Americas Club Championship
- Club World Cup

Rule:
Do not freeze the full competition.
Only lock players while they are live in matches.

## Tournament roster
- min squad = 18
- max squad = 30
- min goalkeepers = 2
- max goalkeepers = 3

## World Cup-style scoring
- role-fit does not apply
- full real-life ratings count
- roster average scoring model applies

## Club mode scoring
- role-fit applies
- starting XI gets full positional scoring
- bench only contributes when used
- captain = 2x
